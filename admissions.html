Admissions

Welcome to Holy Spirit Integrated School's admissions page. We are delighted that you are considering our school for your child's education.

Enrollment Procedures:

  1. Fill out the application form.
  2. Submit the required documents, including birth certificate, report cards, and medical records.
  3. Pay the enrollment fee.
  4. Attend the scheduled interview and entrance examination.
  5. Receive the admission result within the specified timeline.

For further inquiries, please contact our admissions office.
